Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I add a task? — Tap the + button on the Home screen.
  • How do I set a reminder? — Open a task and set a due date with notification enabled.
  • How do I remove ads? — Go to Settings and tap "Remove Ads" to purchase the upgrade.
  • How do I restore a purchase? — Go to Settings and tap "Restore Purchases".
  • Can I sync tasks across devices? — Tasks are stored locally on your device at this time.

Privacy Policy

TaskBoard does not collect or share personal data. Tasks are stored locally on your device and are never uploaded to external servers.

Ads are served by Google AdMob. You may opt out of personalized ads via iOS Settings > Privacy & Security > Apple Advertising.
